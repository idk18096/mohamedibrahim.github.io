About Our Project

The Civil Action Project focuses on understanding the causes of homelessness in our community and finding sustainable solutions. We believe every person deserves dignity, shelter, and hope for a better future.

Our Mission

We aim to educate others about homelessness, volunteer at local shelters, and advocate for stronger housing and support systems. Small actions can lead to a huge impact.

How You Can Help

  • Donate food, clothing, or hygiene products to local shelters.
  • Volunteer your time to help serve meals or support programs.
  • Raise awareness on social media and within your community.
