HUD-Backed Solutions & Programs

Hover or tap a card to learn more about each HUD initiative aimed at reducing homelessness.

Continuum of Care (CoC)

Coordinates housing & services for people experiencing homelessness.

CoC provides local planning, transitional housing, and rapid re-housing grants to stabilize communities.

Emergency Housing Vouchers (EHV)

Moves people quickly into permanent housing.

Short-term rental assistance and housing search support funded by HUD to reduce unsheltered counts.

Homeless Assistance Grants

Funds shelter operations, prevention, and support services.

Stabilizes shelter programs and expands outreach for faster housing placements.

Rapid Re-Housing

Helps households quickly return to stable housing.

Provides rental help, case management, and financial support to prevent homelessness.
